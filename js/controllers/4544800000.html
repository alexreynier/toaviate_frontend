Ireland 2015 - Kent Capri & Ford Club

Fed up with barely visible instruments at night?

Replace the four 3 watt 501 bulbs behind the instrument panel (Mk.3 Capri) with LEDs.

Halford's sell the 501LED for about £10 for a pack of 2, so £20 to do the lot.
You have to get them in the right way round - if you get it wrong, they will not work (but they won't blow up!).

While you are doing the job, get rid of the useless blue domes over each of the bulbs.
